THE INTERNATIONAL SPACE STATION

ISS

.. or the International Space Station, is the largest man made object orbiting the earth. Its a collaborative project between five space agencies: Nasa, Roscosmos, Jaxa, Esa and Csa.

SpaceX is from time to time hired to escort crew and or equipment to and from the space station.
